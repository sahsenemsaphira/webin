01. WHO IS SAPHIRA?

Şahsenem Saphira, known professionally as Saphira, has been releasing music independently since 2019. Her work is defined by controlled vocals and atmospheric production, shaped outside mainstream pop.

Across her releases, Saphira FM serves as a unifying signal, tying her sound together with a clear sense of focus and intent.

02. MUSICAL STYLE

Saphira works within Dark Pop and Alternative R&B, shaping songs around atmosphere rather than structure. Her sound relies on electronic textures and low-end depth, with vocals kept controlled and integrated into the mix
