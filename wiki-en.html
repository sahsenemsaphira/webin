About Sahsenem Saphira

Sahsenem Saphira, known professionally as Saphira, is a 25-year-old German-born musician and mezzo-soprano vocalist. Dominating the dark pop scene with her unique style, she combines high-fashion visuals with cinematic soundscapes.
